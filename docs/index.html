She's navigating something no one prepared her for.

Perimenopause can start in your late 30s. Most women don't see it coming — the brain fog, the sleeplessness, the sudden heat, the anxiety that wasn't there before.

She thought she was alone.

Doctors dismiss it. Partners don't understand. Friends don't talk about it. The silence around menopause is deafening — and it makes everything harder.

She found her people.

Periwink is where thousands of women share what actually works. Real experiences, real data, real support — pseudonymous or fully anonymous. No judgment, ever.

She started tracking. She started healing.

Daily symptom logging revealed patterns she never saw. Community insights showed her she wasn't imagining it. And the treatments others shared? Some of them changed everything.
