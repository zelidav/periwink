About the Founder

Dr. Adrian Tubero, Psy.D.

Clinical Psychologist · Founder, Periwink · New York City

Adrian is a clinical psychologist with over 20 years in private practice, specializing in trauma, attachment, and the deeper emotional patterns that shape how we live and relate. Her work centers on helping people understand themselves more fully — not simply to reduce symptoms, but to create lasting, internal change.

Her own experience navigating perimenopause revealed how fragmented and isolating the landscape of care truly is. What struck her most wasn't just the intensity of the symptoms, but how profoundly this phase affects identity, emotional stability, relationships, and sense of self.

That gap is what led her to build Periwink — a space where women are not reduced to symptoms, but supported as they navigate identity shifts, relationship changes, and the deeper process of becoming more fully themselves. From symptom management to meaning-making. From isolation to connection.

Adrian trained extensively in relational trauma through AEDP and Core Self Reclamation Therapy (CSRT), and is currently deepening her training in women's health through Harvard Medical School's Executive Education program in Women's Health and Wellness.

"This phase of life is not simply a problem to manage — it is a profound psychological and relational experience. With the right support, it can become a path toward greater coherence, clarity, and self-understanding."
